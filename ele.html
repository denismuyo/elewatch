Protecting Elephants Through Community Power

EleWatch empowers communities around Maasai Mara to track elephant movements and prevent human-wildlife conflicts through crowdsourced data collection.

Download the App Join Our Community

How EleWatch Works

Community Reports

Local residents report elephant sightings via the EleWatch app or simple SMS, creating a network of community observers.

Crowdsourced Mapping

Reports are aggregated and displayed on our live map, showing current elephant locations in the Maasai Mara region.

Alerts & Prevention

Automated alerts notify nearby farmers and villages when elephants approach human settlements, preventing conflicts.

Key Features

Real-time Elephant Movement Map

View up-to-date elephant locations in the Maasai Mara based on community reports, with color-coded indicators for recent sightings.

SMS Reporting & Offline Mode

Report sightings via simple SMS without internet access. The app automatically syncs your reports when you're back online.

Customizable Alerts

Receive instant notifications when elephants approach your area with configurable alert zones.

Ranger Coordination

Dedicated interface for rangers to coordinate responses and track elephant herds effectively.

Movement Patterns

Analyze historical elephant movement data to predict future routes and behavior patterns.
